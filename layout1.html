WHAT TYPE OF COFFEE BEAN'S ROAST ARE YOU?

While origin characteristics influence the look, taste and smell of coffee, roast degree impacts the final taste. Light and medium roasted coffees are milder, and manitain much of their origin characteristics. As coffee is roasted darker its origin characteristics become muted, which increases the intensity of flavor.
